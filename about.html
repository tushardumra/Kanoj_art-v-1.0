Meet the Artist

I am Kanoj, a skilled professional sketch artist from Mullanpur, Ludhiana, specialising in portraits, landscapes, conceptual art, and custom artwork. I have been Practising since 2019, and I have developed a realistic style that combines precision with creativity. I am strongly attentive to details. I transform simple ideas into striking visuals that capture emotion and depth. Whether it’s a personal commission or a larger artistic project, my work reflects dedication, craftsmanship, and a passion for bringing stories to life on paper.
